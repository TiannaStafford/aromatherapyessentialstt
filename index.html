ABOUT US


Founded in 2022, Aromatherapy Essentials TT is dedicated to enhancing well-being through the power of aromatherapy. Specializing in air humidifiers, oil diffusers, and our exclusive line of essential oils, we strive to provide natural solutions for anxiety, stress, depression, and common colds.

Operating primarily online, we offer convenient delivery services and have expanded our reach with products available in several pharmacies across Trinidad and Tobago. Our top-selling product, the Cold Reliever oil, is highly effective in alleviating nasal congestion and sinus issues, making it a household favorite.

As one of the esteemed companies under Tsmic Stafford's Group of Companies, we benefit from a strong foundation of excellence and innovation. Over the years, we've grown both in our product range and client base, continually committed to improving lives with our high-quality aromatherapy products. Experience the difference with Aromatherapy Essentials TT, where your well-being is our priority.

OIL DIFFUSERS
An oil diffuser is a device that disperses essential oils into the air, filling a room with their aromatic scent.

Four(4) types of Diffusers: Ultrasonic, Nebulizing, Evaporative and Heat Diffusers.

Learn More about Diffusers Here


AIR HUMIDIFIERS
A humidifier is a device that adds moisture to the air in order to increase humidity levels, and alleviate dryness in the air.

Five(5) types of Humidifiers: Evaporative, Ultrasonic, Steam Vaporizers, Impeller and Central Humidifiers.

Learn More about Humidifiers Here



ESSENTIAL OILS
Essential oils are concentrated extracts derived from plants, capturing their natural fragrance and beneficial properties.

Four(4) types of Essential Oils Uses: Aromatherapy, Topical Application, Household and Personal Care.

Learn More about Essential Oils Here



FRAGRANCES
Fragrances are aromatic compounds used to impart a pleasant scent to various products, including perfumes, cosmetics,and more.

Fragrances help with Mood Enhancement, Stress Relief, Improved Sleep, Respiratory Relief and More.
